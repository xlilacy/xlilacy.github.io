insight into this piece

This piece was inspired by my morning walks through Le Marais, watching the city wake up through the lens of a dreamer.

The recurring motif of strawberries represents the fleeting sweetness of Parisian summers.

Three points that lead to me writing this:

  • Boredom
  • A craving for strawberries
  • A dream I had about Paris
